Cricbuzz

CricBuzz is a sports news website and app to all exclusively for the game of cricket. The site features news, articles, live coverage of cricket matches (including videos and scorecards), player rankings and team rankings. The website also offers many mobile apps.

CricBuzz is also the largest mobile app for cricket news and scores in India. The site was created and is edited by Piyush Agrawal.
